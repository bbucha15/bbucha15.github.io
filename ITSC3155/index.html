About Us

Our goal is to provide data about internet services around your neighborhood or other places you would like to know. Our website checks the internet service percent converage, population, and income. All of these are used to see if they make a difference in the coverage of internet in a location. Our service would be great for internet service provider, business, or just people who want better internet in their location.

This project, made for UNCC ITSC3155, helps visualize the correlation between low income or low population areas to areas underserved by internet providers.
Data is from the Census Bureau and BroadbandNow.
